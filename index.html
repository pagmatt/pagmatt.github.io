Matteo Pagin

I’m a PhD student in the SIGNET group at the Department of Information Engineering of the University of Padova.

I am interested in the modeling, analysis and design of cellular telecommunication networks. In particular, most of my research focuses on the performance evaluation of 5/6G communication networks, primarily using simulation tools such as ns-3. As part of my research endeavors I have been developing and maintaining ns3-mmwave, an ns-3 module for simulating 5G networks and evaluating the performance of novel 5/6G solutions.

I like to spend my free time having aperitif’s with my friends, weightlifting, playing the guitar, hiking and working on side projects.
